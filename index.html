Bio

A native of Tulsa, Oklahoma, Britney is the eldest daughter of Arthur & Tia Presley. Raised in a family of 7 children Britney quickly learned about leadership, patience, and how to deal with different complex personalities. Britney pursued a Track & Field career while attending Oklahoma State University, also taking an entrepreneural route with her own beauty shop. Upon recieving an undergraduate degree with a Bachelors in Universities Studies, the opportunity for Graduate school opened, where Britney then expressed an interest to learn more about Entrepreneurship. To her surprise, just a semester after she started the Entrepreneurship program, got the opportunity to be apart of a fellowship with the Greater Foundation. Based in Seattle, Washington the Greater Foundation created a fellowship to redefine education so that students from all backgrounds are equiped for the opportunities the 21st century presents by connecting the power of sports and technology.
